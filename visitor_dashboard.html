Library Rules

  • Maintain silence inside the library
  • No food or drinks allowed
  • Return borrowed books on time
  • Respect library staff and visitors
  • Mobile phones must be on silent mode
  • Handle books and equipment carefully
  • Group discussions only in designated areas
  • Keep the library clean at all times
  • Report damaged books to library staff
  • Follow library opening and closing hours
  • Do not reserve seats for long periods
  • Follow all library policies and guidelines
